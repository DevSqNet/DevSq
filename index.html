We are a global network of software and hardware developers, designers, product managers, business owners, and users who are working to build a more mobile, connected, and secure future. This is an open community looking to connect, learn, innovate, and collaborate for a more secure, mobile future.

At Developer Square you will...

  • Connect with like minded friends.
  • Learn through online training and workbooks written from the smartest people in the mobile software industry.
  • Innovate on powerful, free software and design practices.
  • Collaborate to build smarter and more secure mobile apps.

Whether you are an average person, business person, parent, a software developer, a user experience designer, or product manager, Developer Square has something for you.

Learn to protect valuable data

In the real world, things gets lost. People are 15 times more likely to lose a mobile phone than a laptop. According to INTEL, consumers on average store "digital assets" to worth 10,000s of dollars in their devices, including digital media, professional information, and personal correspondence and photos — yet more than a third lack protection across all of those devices.

We are creating an online community of people and tools that help prevent the loss of media or privacy. Learn from us on how to stay safe in the real world.
